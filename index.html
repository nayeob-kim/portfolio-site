Hi, I'm Nayeob (나엽 / pronounced Nah-yup), a Product Designer based in Vancouver, Canada.

I specialize in building scalable design systems and complex interfaces that balance high-stakes technical requirements with intuitive user experiences. Having shaped products at Shopify and MetaLab, I've learned that the most successful designs aren't just delightful. They are resilient, accessible, and grounded in a deep understanding of the people using them.

While I leverage AI to accelerate my technical output and prototyping velocity, I believe a designer's true value lies in judgment and empathy. I thrive in unstructured environments, transforming ambiguous business problems into clear, strategic systems by navigating cross-functional trade-offs, leading user advocacy, and maintaining a high bar for product craft.
