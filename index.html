My name is Nayeob (나엽 / pronounced Nah-yub) Kim. I am a Product Designer based in Vancouver, Canada.

 

I focus on solving complex interface challenges through rigorous craft and scalable logic. My experience ranges from driving rapid 0 to 1 product cycles at Metalab to navigating the high-integrity technical requirements of Shopify Checkout.

 

I thrive in environments where trust and accuracy are the priority. I focus on translating ambiguous business needs into reliable products that maintain a high bar for craft and technical rigor. My goal is to bridge the gap between architectural logic and a seamless user experience.
