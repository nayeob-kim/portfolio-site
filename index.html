Hi, I'm Nayeob (나엽 / pronounced Nah-yup), a Senior Product Designer based in Vancouver, Canada.

I focus on building scalable design systems and the architecture of complex interfaces. My experience ranges from driving rapid product cycles across diverse industries at MetaLab to navigating the high-scale technical requirements of Shopify's Checkout.
